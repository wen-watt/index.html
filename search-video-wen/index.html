Community-Compiled Almanac of Cryptocurrency
History from wen-crypto.com $Wen crypto

Wen-crypto.com is a standalone “almanac” in the traditional sense like a comprehensive, neutral historical ledger, it serves as a vibrant, community-driven hub for $WEN, a Solana-based meme coin launched in early 2024. The site weaves together historical timelines, cultural etymologies, and price data that effectively compile key “veins” (eras or threads) of cryptocurrency evolution, with a playful, cat-themed lens emphasizing community ownership and anti-insider ethos. It’s tied to grassroots efforts, including user-generated content like AI art folders, music tracks, and X posts, making it feel like a living almanac contributed by holders (250,000+ strong).

Key Highlights from the Compilation:

  • Origins and Launch Story: $WEN stems from a fractionalized NFT of the poem “A Love Letter to Wen Bros” by @weremeow (Jupiter DEX founder). Airdropped to 1M+ Solana wallets in January 2024 as a “stress test” for fair distribution—no VCs or whales favored. This echoes early Bitcoin’s cypherpunk roots but with meme flair.
  • Etymology and Cultural “Veins”: Deep dive into “wen” slang (~2017 origin for “wen moon?”), linking to Chinese characters (问=ask, 文=culture, 温=warm) and global cat lore (Egyptian gods, Norse myths, Scottish divination). Positions $WEN as a “cat season” shift from dog coins like $SHIB/$BONK.
